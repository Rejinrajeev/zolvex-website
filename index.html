FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our deep cleaning services, booking process, and policies.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. Please contact us with your specific location to confirm service availability in your area.

How do you determine pricing for cleaning services?

Our pricing is based on several factors including the size of the property, type of service required, current condition, and any special requests. We provide free, no-obligation quotes after understanding your specific needs.

What cleaning products do you use?

We use professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products that are effective yet safe for your family, pets, and the environment. We can also accommodate requests for specific products if you have preferences or allergies.

Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?

Yes, all our cleaning professionals are thoroughly vetted, background-checked, and fully insured. Your safety and security are our top priorities.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning service?

It's not necessary for you to be present during the cleaning. Many clients provide us with access instructions or a key. We take security seriously and ensure your property is secure when we leave.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may incur a cancellation fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you in those situations.

Do you offer any satisfaction guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our service, please contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to address your concerns at no additional cost.
